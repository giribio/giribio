### 🧬 About Me
I am a Computational Scientist in India with over 15 years of experience in computational drug discovery. My work focuses on the intersection of **AI and Life Sciences**, specifically in finding "undruggable" targets, target identification and advancing longevity research.

- 🔭 **Current Focus:** Agentic AI for Life, BioAI, Target Identification, Longevity, and Cryptic Pockets.
- 🌱 **Learning Path:** Advanced Deep Learning with **PyTorch** & **TensorFlow**, and Generative AI for Drug Discovery.
- 👯 **Collaboration:** Open to projects involving **BioAI workflows**, **Target Identification**, and **ML-based SAR models**.
- 🤝 **Mission:** Driving longevity studies focused on the **Indian Population**.
- 💬 **Ask me about:** MD Simulations (NAMD/GROMACS), QSAR, KNIME Workflows, and Agentic AI.
- ⚡ **Fun fact:** I talk a lot because I’m passionate about motivating the next generation of scientists!
